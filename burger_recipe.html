Burger Ingredients

When trying to make the perfect vegan burger, it's all about the ingredients as it is about the techniques. Finding the best fresh vegetables, the best vegan burger patty and placing it on the BBQ for the right amount. This is what makes the best vegan burger.

Burger Patty

When it comes to the burger patty, it's better to look out for something vegan. If you do that you can get a very reliable source of protein that actually taste like a real burger. Some people prefer to make a burger patty out of beans, lentils or chickpeas. If you are would rather have a legumes burger istead, that's totally fine even though, it will not taste like a real burger anymore.

Burger Bun

Not much to say about burger buns. Except that they are also as important as the rest of the ingredients.

Feel free to choose the ones that suit you best, based on your preferences.

What to look after when you are choosing your burger bun:

  • Color: Make sure is very well done by looking at the color. Is should have a golden shade.
  • Texture:It should look very fluffy
  • Nutrition factors:proteins, carbs and sugars are very important.
  • Condition:make sure it is as fresh as possible

Burger Asamblance

for assembling your burger, make sure that the vegan patty is well done. You can also add the bun to a pan for a minute or two, to give it a beautiful golder color.

Next, place the patty at the base of your burger when it is still hot, so that when you place the cheese on top of it, it will melt. Moving on to the veggies. Make sure that they are clean and that they are cutted in a pretty way. Then you add your 1000 island sauce and there you go! Enjoy your vegan burger.
